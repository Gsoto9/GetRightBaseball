Services

At Get Right Training, we specialize in personalized baseball and softball coaching tailored to players of all ages and skill levels. From foundational techniques to advanced strategies, our programs are designed to enhance every aspect of your game. Whether you’re looking to improve your swing, refine your pitching, or boost your speed and agility, our experienced trainers are here to guide you every step of the way.

Beyond skill development, we also emphasize physical conditioning and mental preparedness. Our training approach combines cutting-edge techniques with a supportive environment to help athletes build confidence and reach their full potential. Whether you're a beginner aiming to master the basics or an advanced player preparing for the next level, we’ll help you achieve your goals and excel on the field.

Meet our Coaches

Tyler Thomas

Owner / Coach at Get Right Training

I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona, and ultimately found my passion coaching here in Bismarck, North Dakota! I have grown up around the sport of baseball and softball my whole life, having played baseball myself and a younger sister playing softball and coaching her growing up! I grew up playing on many teams, as baseball is year-round down south, and those teams include, but are not limited to, Catalina Foothills High School, Tucson Prospects, BNL, and the University of Mary! The University of Mary is where I played NCAA Division II collegiate baseball and where I ultimately ended my baseball career but found my newest journey! Bismarck introduced me to coaching, as I’ve been coaching for the past 4 and a half years, both in Bismarck and Arizona! These last few years of working with children up to adults with the game of baseball and softball have made me fall in love with not only the game but the aspects of coaching! Now that you are done hearing about some of me, I would love nothing more than to meet everyone in the Bismarck/Mandan and surrounding communities down at our facility and talk about what we have to offer in order to grow these sports around the state of North Dakota!

Dylan Thomas

Owner / Coach at Get Right Training

I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona surrounded by multiple sports, but fell in love with the game of baseball at a young age. Work ethic and self accountability are at the top of my list in regard to my coaching philosophy. I’ve seen just how far 10,000 hours of work can shape an athlete. Whether those hours take place in the gym, the diamond, or in the classroom putting in the work will evolve you in many facets. There are no equal outcomes, but as coaches we can help make the hard work fun and help our athletes become better players, and more importantly better people. So when they enter the world as adults they’re ready for whatever is thrown their way.
